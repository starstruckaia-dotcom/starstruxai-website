Live Build — Ottawa, ON
An Ottawa PM firm. 250+ units.
One 8-hour day, every month.

An 11-person property management company in Ottawa's Golden Triangle was processing over 250 utility bills manually each month — logging into separate portals per account, downloading PDFs, coding invoices by property, and entering data into Yardi. The process took a full workday. Every month. Without fail.

We automated the entire pipeline: bill retrieval via email monitoring, Claude AI-powered PDF extraction, property-level categorization, deduplication, and a Yardi-ready CSV output. The admin now spends 20 minutes reviewing exceptions instead of 8 hours processing everything.
